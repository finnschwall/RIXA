What is RIXA?

A research project.
...We present our concept and work in progress implementation of a new kind of XAI dashboard that uses a natural language chat. We specify 5 design goals for the dashboard and show the current state of our implementation. The natural language chat is the main form of interaction for our new dashboard. Through it the user should be able to control all important aspects of our dashboard...
